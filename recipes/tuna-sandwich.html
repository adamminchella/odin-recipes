Tuna Sandwich

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make the most delicious tuna mayo sandwich in the world, Minchella style!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices Bread
  • 1 tin Tuna
  • 1/2 tin Sweetcorn
  • Lots of Mayo
  • A bit of Cucumber

Steps

  1. Slice bread
  2. Drain the tuna and sweetcorn
  3. Combine tuna, mayo and sweetcorn
  4. Mix well with a fork
  5. Dollop tuna mix onto one slice of bread
  6. Slice cucumber
  7. Put cucumber onto tuna mix
  8. Place other slice on top and enjoy!
